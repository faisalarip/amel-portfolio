About

A strategist who turns numbers into narratives — and narratives into revenue.

I'm Amel, a Sales & Marketing leader currently heading commercial strategy at PT Elpix Global Vision (Jasafotojakarta), where I was promoted from Specialist to Head of Sales & Marketing in under three years.
